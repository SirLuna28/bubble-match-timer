## App Description (Long)

Match colorful bubbles before time runs out in this fast-paced cosmic puzzle adventure! Bubble Match Timer combines addictive match-3 gameplay with intense timer mechanics for an experience that keeps you coming back.

**Features:**
- **100 Challenging Levels**: Progressive difficulty from easy to extreme, with dynamically scaling bubble sizes and speeds
- **Power-Up System**: Unlock and use strategic power-ups including Time-Slow, Sticking Bubble, and Bomb Bubble to overcome tough levels
- **Gameplay-Based Rewards**: Earn power-ups through level completion, score achievements, and combo streaks
- **Global Leaderboard**: Compete with players worldwide and climb the rankings
- **Cosmic Aesthetics**: Stunning neon visuals with immersive sound effects and haptic feedback
- **Mobile Optimized**: Perfectly designed for iOS with safe-area compliance and smooth 60fps gameplay
- **100% Ad-Free**: Completely free to play with zero advertisements
- **No In-App Purchases**: All content earned through gameplay

**Gameplay:**
Drag bubbles to create matches of 3 or more of the same color. Each match pops the bubbles and earns you points. Race against the clock to reach your level goal before time expires. Use power-ups strategically to clear tough boards and advance to the next level. Retry levels unlimited times with no penalties.

**Reward System:**
Earn power-ups naturally as you progress through levels. Achieve high scores to unlock bonus rewards. Build combo streaks for additional power-ups. All rewards are earned through gameplay—no ads, no purchases, no grinding.

**Perfect For:**
- Casual gamers looking for quick gaming sessions
- Puzzle enthusiasts who love match-3 games
- Players seeking a challenge with progressive difficulty
- Anyone who enjoys vibrant, modern game aesthetics
- Players who prefer ad-free gaming experiences

Download Bubble Match Timer today and join millions of players in the cosmic puzzle adventure!
